<div class="callout">
<strong>CAIO</strong> (City–AI Coevolution) studies how AI-driven services—navigation, ride-hailing, house renting, and location-based recommenders—co-adapt with urban environments through feedback loops.
</div>

## Research pillars
<div class="grid">
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Understand</h3>
    <p>Measure the impacts of AI-driven recommenders on mobility, sustainability, and inequality.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Model</h3>
    <p>Develop mechanistic models of the city–AI feedback loop.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Govern</h3>
    <p>Design complexity-informed recommenders aligned with collective urban goals.</p>
  </div>
</div>
